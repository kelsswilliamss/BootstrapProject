Favorite Food Spots in Savannah

A site for 5 of my favorite food spots in Savannah, Georgia. Are there more than this? Oh, absolutely, but here's just a few.

Collin's Quarter

Looking for a nice brunch spot with indoor and outdoor seating? Collin's Quarter is where you want to be! This restauratn also serves dinner and has the most beautiful decor. I have never met such a friendly staff as well. From the welcome, to the servers, and even the clean up, everything about this place is lovely. I highly recommed the Banana Foster French Toast if you are going for brunch.

Treylor Park

A fun and light hearted place for lunch and dinner. The waffle fry nachos, a must have. There are several locations for this one, but my favorite is on Bay Street near the river.

Previous Next

The Fat Radish

This restaurant is a little more on the upscale end, but pricing wise, still affordale! Believe it or not, this is actually a seafood place. My favorite dish is the crab mac and cheese. So so good! A must try!

Previous Next

Savannah Coffee Roasters

Savannah Coffee Roasters is great place if you're on the go, want to sit for a meal, or even just hang out and do some work. The coffee is amazing, the food is great, and the servers are so kind!

Previous Next

Mirabelle Cafe

Mirabelle Cafe is the perfect spot for a quick pick me up when out walking through the shops. They're waffles are to die for and made fresh to order. The smores and birthday options are so so yummy.
